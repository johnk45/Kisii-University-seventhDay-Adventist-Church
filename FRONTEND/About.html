About Kisii University SDA Church (KSUSDA)

Kisii University SDA Church (KSUSDA) is a Christ-centered community dedicated to spiritual growth, service, and excellence. Our mission is to nurture faith among students and staff through worship, evangelism, and service while preparing them to be lights of hope in the world.

Our Mission

To proclaim the everlasting gospel of Jesus Christ to all members of the university community and to prepare disciples who live according to His Word through worship, fellowship, and service.

Our Vision

To be a spiritually empowered and united body of believers who reflect the character of Christ and serve the world with compassion, integrity, and faithfulness.

Our Values

We are guided by faith, love, unity, humility, and service. Our values inspire us to be good stewards of time, knowledge, and opportunities God provides.
