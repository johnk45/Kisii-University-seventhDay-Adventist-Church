What We Believe

At Kisii University Seventh-day Adventist Church, our faith is built upon the eternal Word of God. We believe in living by Christ’s teachings — growing in love, serving with compassion, and preparing for His soon return.

1. God the Creator

We believe in one God — Father, Son, and Holy Spirit — a unity of three co-eternal Persons. God is love, the Creator and Sustainer of all things, worthy of worship and praise.

2. The Word of God

The Scriptures, both Old and New Testaments, are the written Word of God, inspired by the Holy Spirit. They reveal His will and guide us in truth and righteousness.

3. The Sabbath

The seventh day is the Sabbath, a day of rest and worship established at creation. It reminds us of God’s power as our Creator and Redeemer, calling us to communion with Him.

4. The Second Coming of Christ

We look forward to the soon return of our Lord Jesus Christ — a glorious event when He will restore all things, bringing everlasting peace to His faithful followers.

5. Christian Living

As followers of Christ, we are called to reflect His love through service, humility, and holiness — walking daily in grace and sharing the light of the gospel.

Living Our Faith

Our beliefs call us to reflect the character of Christ in all we do—at school, at home, and in our community. We invite you to learn, worship, and serve with us as we grow in faith together.
